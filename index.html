Welcome to Rwanda

Rwanda, officially the Republic of Rwanda, is a landlocked country in Central Africa. It is bordered by Uganda, Tanzania, Burundi, and the Democratic Republic of the Congo1. With a population of over 13 million people, Rwanda is hilly, fertile, and located a few degrees south of the Equator, get a snapshot about it here here.

City of Kigali

Kigali, Rwanda's capital, enchants with its lush hills, vibrant culture, and modern charm. The city's clean streets and scenic beauty create a captivating atmosphere.
